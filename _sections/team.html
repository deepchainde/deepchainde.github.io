Supply Chain - Trustworthy product tracing to fight against counterfeiting and pirating is a challenging task. Legacy implementations are vulnerable for both insider misuse and external attacks. Trustworthy Product Tracing (TPT) is a blockchain-based platform to provide reliable product traceability for all parties involved in the product lifecycle.

Healthcare - Privacy-aware healthcare data sharing is critical for patients who receive medical care from multiple doctors and hospitals. Existing solutions rely heavily on complex cyber security measures to ensure the compliance with given regulations. Privacy-aware healthcare Data Sharing (PDS) is a private permissioned blockchain system based on Hyperledger Fabric offering end-to-end encryption and trustworthy data usage history.

Energy - Peer-to-peer renewable energy trading is essential to minimize the overhead of long-distance energy transmission and distribution. Centralized energy economy and the corresponding trading model will get updated with the increasingly faster deployment of renewable energy facilities. Peer-to-peer renewable Energy Trading (PET) is a marketplace where renewable energy generator and consumer can trade with each other without a middleman in between.

Enterprise Digitalization - Digitalization plays a increasingly significant role in today's enterprise context. Functionality, usability, and security are three key factors for the deployment of a successful enterprise application. We provide individual consulting and development service for small and medium enterprises to optimally digitalize their business processes with state-of-the-art IT technology.
